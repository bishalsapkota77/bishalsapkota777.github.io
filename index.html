About me

I am Bishal Sapkota, born in a tiny town located at the base of Jugal Mountain of Nepal on March 2004. Since an early age, I was raised on Kathmandu, the capital city of Nepal where I pursued my high school degree. I was a creative kid since my childhood who used to ponder on the nature, who always want to know how the things on the earth works. As I spent lot of my time on the nature; travelling around different place, capturing photos and videos, learning several new skills and learning about various cultures became my field of interest. My hobbies includes reading books, weight-lifting, listening to classical songs, photography. According to my mom, I am strong, I am capable, and I can achieve anything I set on my mind. She believes in me and so do I.
